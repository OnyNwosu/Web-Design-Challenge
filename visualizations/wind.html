City Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The majority of wind speeds are almost evenly distributed at latitudes between -40 and 70, with with speeds between 0 and 15 mph. Most of the wind speeds are present above the equator. Latitudes of 45 to 80, have wind speeds over 25 mph, with the highest wind speed recording at a latitude of about 48.
